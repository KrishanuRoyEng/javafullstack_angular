The most crucial way to understand this connection is through the "is-a" relationship. 
Inheritance should only be used if this relationship makes logical sense.
   .A Car is-a Vehicle.
   .A Truck is-a Vehicle.
   
   .A Dog is-a Animal.
   .A Cat is-a Animal.
   
   
   .A SavingsAccount is-a BankAccount.
   .A CurrentAccount is-a BankAccount.
   
If you can't say "X is-a Y" naturally, then inheritance is probably the wrong tool. 
This relationship ensures your code models the real world logically.
