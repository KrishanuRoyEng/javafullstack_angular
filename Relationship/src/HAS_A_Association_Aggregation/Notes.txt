This is a broad category where one object has a reference to another object. 
It's the most common relationship and is the basis for composition and aggregation.

a) Aggregation (Weak HAS-A)
Concept: A "whole-part" relationship where the part can exist independently of the whole. 
         It's a weak association.

Keyword: new (usually created outside and passed in)
A Department has Professors, but a Professor can exist without a Department.
